About Us

CV. Mitra Tata Usaha was established in 2020, located in Mojokerto, Indonesia. Initially, we supplied and sold molasses products and industrial salt/iodized salt for local industries in Indonesia. In its development, we see a potential international market demand for several products originating from Indonesia. CV. Mitra Tata Usaha become a trade and supply company for Coco Fiber, Rattan Handicraft, Wood Pellet, Sulfur Stone products. Our promise is to market and supply quality local Indonesian products worldwide and to help local companies we work with to reach international markets. We have connected with many business partners and many employees.

Vision

Helping companies that work with “CV. Mitra Tata Usaha” to maximize sales, and meet the needs of buyers on a national and international scale so that the selling value of goods is better.

Mission

As a market place to bring together prouden and buyers on a national and international scale.

Iodium salt

Iodized salt is one of the food intakes that need to be consumed daily. Iodized salt has many health benefits, including preventing thyroid disease and maintaining the health of pregnant women and the fetus in the womb. Iodized salt is salt that has been fortified or added mineral iodine. Iodine is used to help the body produce thyroid hormones, which are hormones that play a role in regulating the body's metabolic processes and various organ functions in the body. Specification Product :
